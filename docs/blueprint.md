# **App Name**: The Mind of a Woman Developer

## Core Features:

- Immersive Cosmic Background: The experience begins in darkness with subtle cosmic particles drifting slowly across the background, creating an immersive cosmic ambiance.
- Neural Constellation Animation: Glowing particles gather toward the center and gradually form a futuristic, elegant brain-shaped neural constellation from particles and circuit lines upon initial load.
- Sequential Node Illumination: Five key nodes (Logic, Creativity, Empathy, Collaboration, Curiosity) appear one by one around the brain, guiding the viewer's attention.
- Dynamic Node Insights: When a viewer hovers over a node, the node expands slightly, its glow intensifies, and a short insight appears describing how that quality contributes to technology and innovation.
- Node Connection Pulses: Light pulses travel between nodes across the neural network, visually representing how innovation emerges from interconnected thinking.

## Style Guidelines:

- A dark cosmic background with a premium neon palette of blue, violet, pink, cyan, and soft gold for glowing lines, particles, and gradients, creating a modern and cinematic aesthetic.
- 'Space Grotesk' is used for a futuristic, precise, and readable typographic identity.
- The brain should be futuristic, elegant, conceptual, and artistic, resembling a digital constellation mixed with a neural network and circuit board. Nodes are minimalist geometric forms with subtle glows.
- Central composition with the neural constellation as the focal point, utilizing an immersive fullscreen canvas with no obstructive UI elements. Designed for responsive adaptation across devices.
- Framer Motion orchestrates sequential animations for the brain formation, node illumination, dynamic hover interactions (expansion, glow intensification), and subtle background particle movement, ensuring smooth performance.
